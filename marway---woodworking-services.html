Our products

Marway Ltd is a family-run business that has been involved in furniture production since 2009.

We offer a wide range of high-quality wooden products, including furniture, doors, saunas, garden furniture, and other wooden items.

We produce small, medium, and large batches for both the Latvian and export markets, and we also provide customized solutions. We specialize in the production of children's furniture.

We use a variety of top-quality materials, including wood (birch, alder, oak, ash, pine, aspen, beech, heat-treated wood), MDF, plywood, laminate, metal, and glass. Additionally, we offer various types of woodworking services.
